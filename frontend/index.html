FAQ

Questions,
answered

How does the resume analysis work?

We use natural language processing (spaCy) to read your PDF resume, extract skills, education, and experience, then match them against a structured skills taxonomy. The whole process takes under ten seconds and nothing is shared with third parties.

Are the recommended courses really free?

Yes. Every resource in your roadmap — courses, tutorials, and embedded videos — is completely free. We curate from platforms like freeCodeCamp, YouTube, Khan Academy, and MIT OpenCourseWare.

How accurate are the career match scores?

Match scores are computed with TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity between your skill profile and each career's requirements. Scores are fully explainable — you can always see exactly which skills contributed and which are missing.

What happens after I finish a learning module?

You take a short quiz to verify the skill. Passing marks it as mastered on your profile, updates your analytics, and unlocks the next stage of your roadmap. If you don't pass, we suggest targeted revision resources.

Is my data secure?

Your account is protected with JWT authentication and bcrypt-hashed passwords. Your resume is processed for analysis and never sold, shared, or used for anything other than generating your recommendations.
